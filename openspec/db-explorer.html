Ví dụ đánh giá ABAC — user "an.nguyen" gọi GET /api/employee/list lúc 16:45 thứ 4
Step 1 JWT token → load user id=2 (an.nguyen)
Step 2 RBAC check: user có EMPLOYEE_READ (qua ROLE_MANAGER) → ✅ pass
Step 3 @abacEval.isAllowed(auth, "EMPLOYEE", null) được gọi
Step 4 Resolve department: user.employee_id=1 → employee.department_id=1 (IT)
Step 5 Query: SELECT * FROM access_policies WHERE resource_type='EMPLOYEE'
AND subject_type='DEPARTMENT' AND subject_id=1 AND enabled=true
Step 6 Found policy id=1: day=ALL, 08:00–17:00, effect=ALLOW
Step 7 Match day? ALL → ✅ | Match time? 16:45 ∈ [08:00,17:00] → ✅
Step 8 anyAllow=true, no DENY found → GRANT ACCESS ✅

Scenario B Nếu gọi lúc 18:30:
Step 7b Match time? 18:30 ∉ [08:00,17:00] → policy không match
Step 8b anyAllow=false, policies exist → throw AccessTimeException → 403 FORBIDDEN ❌
